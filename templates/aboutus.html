About Campus Cupboard

Our Mission

The Augustana College Campus Cupboard provides non-perishable food items and hygiene products to the student body. All Augustana students are welcome to take items from the cupboard.

We are dedicated to fighting food insecurity among our student community. We provide free food and essential items to all Augustana students in need, no questions asked.

How It Works

Students can visit the Campus Cupboard during regular hours to select items they need. Our inventory is updated regularly and supported by donations from the community and local partners.

Check our Inventory Feed to see what's currently available.

Get Involved

There are many ways to support the Campus Cupboard:

  • Volunteer your time
  • Donate food or essential items
  • Spread the word to students who might need our services
