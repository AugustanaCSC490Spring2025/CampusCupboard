About Us

How Campus Cupboard Works

Students can visit the Campus Cupboard during regular hours to select items they need. Our inventory is updated regularly and supported by donations from the community and local partners. Check our Inventory Feed to see what's currently available.

Our Mission

To ensure no Augustana student goes hungry by providing free access to food and essential items, creating a supportive and dignified environment where students can thrive in their academic pursuits.

Our Vision

To create a campus community where every student has reliable access to nutritious food, allowing them to focus on their education without the burden of food insecurity.

Our Services

Food Assistance

  • Non-perishable food items
  • Hygiene products
  • Basic necessities
