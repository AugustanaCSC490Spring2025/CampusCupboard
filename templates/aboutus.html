About Us

How Campus Cupboard Works

Students can visit the Campus Cupboard during regular hours to select items they need. Our inventory is updated regularly and supported by donations from the community and local partners.

How Campus Closet Works

Students can visit the Campus Closet during the same regular hours and location as the Cupboard. The Campus Closet provides clothing and takes donations.
Check our Inventory Feed to see what's currently available.

Our Mission

To ensure no Augustana student goes hungry by providing free access to food, clothing, and essential items, creating a supportive and dignified environment where students can thrive in their academic pursuits.

Our Vision

To create a campus community where every student has reliable access to nutritious food and clothing, allowing them to focus on their education without the burden of food insecurity and basic necessities.

Our Services

Food Service

  • Non-perishable food items
  • Hygiene products
  • Basic necessities

Clothing Service

  • Provides donated clothing
  • Takes Donations
  • Open to all students
